A discussion on oranges: Oranges are an inferior fruit. The effort required to peel, membrane-like texture, and bursts of citrus shooting in all directions make eating an orange a terrible experience.
A discussion on grapes: Grapes are mediocre. Bite-sized bites of sweet and tart flavors are perfect for any occasion. However, the downfall of grapes is that they are not always good. Sometimes you end up with a pound of dirt-tasting fruit.
A discussion on apples: Apples are a perfect anytime snack. They have so many varities that there is a type for everone. From sweet to sour, crisp to soft, apples are the superior fruit.
